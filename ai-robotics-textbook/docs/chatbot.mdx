## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be Specific**: Ask detailed questions for more targeted answers
2. **Use Technical Terms**: The chatbot understands robotics terminology
3. **Ask Follow-ups**: You can ask follow-up questions in the same conversation
4. **Select Text**: Choose specific passages to ask focused questions
5. **Check Sources**: Review the retrieved documents to understand the answer

## Example Questions

- What are degrees of freedom in robotics?
- How does forward kinematics work?
- Explain the difference between forward and inverse kinematics
- What are the key components of a humanoid robot?
- How do neural networks help with object detection?
- What is Qdrant used for in this project?
- Explain bipedal locomotion

Feel free to ask any questions about the content!
